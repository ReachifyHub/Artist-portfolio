Biography

Harmony Noir is a genre-defying musician who merges traditional classical training with contemporary electronic production. Born in Vienna and trained at the prestigious Juilliard School, their musical journey took an unexpected turn when they discovered the mesmerizing possibilities of electronic soundscapes.

With over a decade of professional experience, Harmony has composed for major films, collaborated with world-renowned artists, and performed at venues ranging from intimate jazz clubs to massive music festivals.

Their work has been described as "a bridge between centuries" by Rolling Stone and "the future of classical music" by Pitchfork.
